Creamy Mushroom Noodles

A college student altered recipe of Chef Perry's Mac and Cheese. This recipe assumes the chef to be broke and on a budget. Feel free to replace certain aspects with other alternatives. Different protein like day old chicken wings or uneaten hamburger patties can be substituted. Free vegetables from college food programs and other "sources" can be used instead of leeks. Other unused pastas and soups can be used instead of the instant ramen. Any spices from the communal kitchen is encouraged to be appropriated and used.

  • Noodels (any at hand)
  • Vegetable (brocoli is used here)
  • Tofu or any other protein
  • Milk
  • Flour, potato starch, or any other kind of emulsifer
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  1. Add butter to a pot over medium heat until melted.
  2. Add in flour and whisk until no lumps.
  3. Add in cold milk 1 cup at a time and whisk quickly until mixed. Adjust until slightly less than desired consistency.
  4. Add in cheese and whisk until thickened. Adjust to desired consistency.
  5. Add in pasta, salt, pepper, and whatever spice at hand.
